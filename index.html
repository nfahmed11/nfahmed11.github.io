About Me

Hello, World! I'm excited to have caught your eye! While I have your attention, allow me to borrow a moment of your time.

I am a multidisciplinary front-end web developer with an art and design background. In other words, I went to art school and came out a programmer.

OOPS!

My interest in web development started as a side hobby with a few personal projects, creating for small businesses. Then I did a little bootcamping, and a few other courses. Now here I am... And here you are.

Aside from coding, I like to spend my free time leveling up my other life skills. I enjoy weight lifting, creating music/art, and DIY projects here and there. I try to be a well rounded person - A jack of all trades if I may.
